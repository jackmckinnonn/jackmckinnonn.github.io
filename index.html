Past, Present, and Future Wide Receivers of the NFL

Stats, Stories, Highlights, etc.

The wide receiver position is one of the most iconic in all of football. Wide receivers are among the most notable players in NFL history.

Some notable wide receivers of the NFL include names like Jerry Rice, Randy Moss, Calvin Johnson, Justin Jefferson, etc.

Jerry Rice holds most of the NFL's receiving records, including:
  • 22,895 career receiving yards
  • 197 career touchdowns
  • 1,549 career receptions
